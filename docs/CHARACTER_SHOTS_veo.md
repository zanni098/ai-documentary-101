## REUSABLE "TALKING" CLIPS (the workhorses — 8–10s each, subtle looping motion)

- **R3 — WIDE / POWER** — full body, desert; behind him: the black helicopter, **two armed guards**, and **two black SUVs**, heat haze and dust. He gestures with both hands as if laying it all out. *(authority / establishing)*
- **R4 — 3/4 TURN** — he's looking off across the dunes, then turns to camera and gestures. *(transitions between exhibits)*
- **R5 — LOW ANGLE** — camera low, looking up, he stands tall, slow, menacing, minimal motion. *(the "one agent" reveal beat)*
- **R6 — HANDS DETAIL** — tight on gloved hands gesturing / adjusting a cuff / a slow open-handed reveal. *(cutaway for variety, hides any weirdness)*
- **R7 — SLOW WALK** — he walks slowly across / toward camera through the sand, talking, coat moving. *(movement variety)*
- **R8 — GOLDEN HOUR WIDE** — low sun, very long shadows across dunes, he stands still, contemplative. *(the reflection scene)*
